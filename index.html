Curious about Uganda?

1. Uganda's population is around 41 million people. Most of these people are below the age of 30.
2. Our land area on the hand is quite small (241,038km²)compared to our population.
If you take Oregon (state in USA) which has roughly the same land area, their population is roughly 4.1 million.
3. For every tree you cut, the law requires that you plant 3.
4. Of the 700 or so mountain gorillas left in the world, more than half are in Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. The rest are on the borders Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda, and Uganda.
5. 11% of the world's bird population is in Uganda. This translates to 1,061 different bird species that can be found in Uganda.
