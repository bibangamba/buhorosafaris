Buhoro

In my mother tongue, Buhoro means "Peace" first and foremost. This shows that Uganda is a peaceful country at it's core (people and culture).
The other reason I love and therefore chose Buhoro, is my Mother's name is Kyobuhoro. So, Buhoro really means a lot to me.

Why I set up this website

Whenever I meet new people, especially in the Benelux, most of them do not know about Uganda. And those that do know Uganda do not know the depth of it's beauty. I want to change that.
I wanted to create a site where I could share the beauty of Uganda and act as a link between the people I meet and the best companies that organise tours, activities, community based tourism events, etc. so that they can experience the best of Uganda.
