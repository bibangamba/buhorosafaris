Why we created this website

Whenever I meet new people and tell them I'm Ugandan, the first thing that comes to mind uis usually negative. Either something they read about in the news recently or something that is from the dark history we've had. I want to change that.
We have so much beauty, kindness and hospitality back home but all that gets buried under history or the occasional negative news. I wanted to create a site where I could share the beauty of Uganda and act as a link between the people/new friends I meet and the different companies that organise tours, activities, community based tourism events, etc.

— Sandra
